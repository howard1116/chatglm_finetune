Abstract: The cationic /nonionic waterborne epoxy esters were synthesized from epoxy , tall
oil fatty acid , polyether amine M2070 , N Ndimethyl1 3propanediamine ( DMPDA) and
diethanolamine ( DEA) . The effects of the cationic /nonionic hydrophilic monomer on the basic
properties , particle size and distribution of the emulsion and the properties of the coating were
studied. The results showed that the emulsion exhibited optimal performance , meanwhile , its
film showed high hardness up to H adhesion 1 , water resistance 168 h , acid resistance 36 h ,
alkali resistance 36 h and impact resistance up to 50 cm when the cation content was 32 mmol/
3.5%
