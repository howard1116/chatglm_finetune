Abstract: A polyurethane prepolymer modified epoxy resin was prepared from isophorone
disocyanate( IPDI), polyethyleneglycol( PEC) and epoxy resin E51 , and was further used
as an emulsifier to emulsify E51 to form a nonionic waterborne epoxy =polyurethane emulsion
via the phase inversion technique. The structure of the prepared emulsion was characterized by
FTIR , furthermore , the particle size and morphology of the emulsion were characterized by
nano particle size analyzer and transmission
 electron microcopy ( TEM) . The influence of a-
mount of polyurethane prepolymer and the molecular weight of PEG on the emulsion stability
and the particle size was discussed. The experiment results showed that when the molecular
weight of PEG was 6 000 , the amount of polyurethane prepolymer was 20% waterbome epoxy
polyurethane emulsion exhibited good comprehensive performance with the emulsion particle
size less than 150 nm.

预聚体改性的环氧树脂在乳化环氧树脂的过程中聚
氨醋预聚体用量较低时没有足够的乳化剂将环氧树
脂表面完全覆盖因而这层界面膜强度不够树脂分
子间发生碰撞而形成较大液滴形成的水性乳液粒径
尺寸较大易发生聚结。随着聚氨酯预聚体用量的增
大能够达到体系所需要的临界胶束浓度时环氧树
脂表面被完全覆盖形成水包油体系这样所得乳液
乳胶粒数目多粒径小因此其乳液的稳定性也大大
提高，继续增加聚氨酯预聚体的用量超过临胶束浓
度以后过量的乳化剂分布于水相中不再起到乳化
的作用反而会增大乳液的浓度从而导致乳液粒径
变大。故聚氨酯预聚体用量为20%较合适。
