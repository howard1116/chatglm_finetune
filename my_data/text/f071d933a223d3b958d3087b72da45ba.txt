Abstract ; In order to simplify the purification process of tung oilbased alcohol for prepa-
ration of highquality tung oilbased polyurethane , the tung oil based polyurethane (TOBP)
was prepared through a first transesterification reaction of tung oil and methanol forming tung
oil based fatty acid methyl ester ( ME) , which was then esterified (second transestrification)
with 1 , 4 butanediol forming the tung oil based alcohol (TOBA). It was finally reacted with
isophorone disocyanate to synthesis the tung oil based polyurethane. The structure and proper-
ties of the product were characterized by fourier transform infrared spectroscopy (FTIR ) and
thermogravimetric analyzer (TGA). The results showed that the synthesized material was the
target product , the content of tung oil based alcohol was increased , and the heat resistance , wa-
ter resistance , tensile strength and hardness of cured film were improved , but the elongation at
break reduced slightly. The prepared tung oilbased polyurethane conformed to the require-
ments of national standard for general primer used in interior decorative solventborne polyure
thane wood coatings.
