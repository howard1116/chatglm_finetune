Abstract: One kind of hydroxyl waterborme polyurethane is synthesized after neutralization and dispersant with water of the
reactions with the polyester diols(PE-1000), dimethylolpropionic acid(DMPA), 1,6-hexanediol(HDO), trimethylolpropane(TMP)
and isophorone diisocyanate (IPDl). Flexible waterborne wood clearcoat is made by the prepared resin, Bayhydrol
XP2655,defoaming agent, leveling agent, additives and solvents. The properties of the emulsion and its film were
characterized by Fourier transform infrared spectroscopy (FT-IR), differential scanning calorimetry(DSC),thermal gravimetric
analyzer(TGA),particle size analyzer and tensile test machine and other equipment. Test results indicated when the molecular
weight of the resin was designed for 5 000 to 6 000, the total amount of DMPA from 4.5% to 5.0%, the content of HDO from
8% to 10%, the content of TMP from 5.0% to 5.5%, neutralization degree at about 1.0, the prepared resin has low particle size,
viscosity and water absorption rate with shelf life above one year. The prepared waterborme wood clearcoat has good properties
e and alcohol resistance
Key words: waterbome polyurethane with hydroxyl groups, waterbome wood clearcoat, softness
