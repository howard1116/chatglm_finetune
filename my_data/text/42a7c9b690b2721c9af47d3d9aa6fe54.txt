Abstract: A high solid content waterborne polyurethane ( WPU) with low viscosity was
synthesized from poly( 1 4butylene adipate) glycol( PBA1000) and polytetramethylene gly-
col( PTMG  100O) as soft segments , isophorone diisocyanate as hard segments , dimethylol
propionicacid( DMPA) as hydrophilic chain extender. Then the WPU was modified with y*
aminopropyltriethoxysilane ( KH550) . The emulsion and film of WPU was analyxzed by rota-
tional viscometer , dynamic light scattering tester , electronic universal tensile machine ,
Fourier transform infrared spectrum , dynamic mechanical thermal analyses , thermo gravimetric
analysis and atomic force microscope. The mechanism of KH550 modified WPU was also ana-
lyzed. When the content of KH55O was 2 wt% of the prepolymer , the modified WPU exhibi-
ted the optimal comprehensive performance. Specifically , the solid content of the WPU was a-
round 50%. The emulsion particle size was less than 130 nm. The viscosity was less than 400
mPa * s. The water absorption of the film was less than 5%, The tensile strength was more than
35 MPa. The elongation at break was more than 750%

越来越广泛地应用于室内装修、皮革涂饰、胶粘剂等

20%-40%。由于固含量较低存在产品的运输和贮
存成本高、初粘性低、成膜速度慢、能耗高等缺点。
在现有的对水性聚氨酯的研究报道中稳定性好、固

乳液的分散性，但是同时也降低了材料的耐水性。

的丙酮和KH-550并升温至回流温度下反应1h。降
温至40℃C用TEA中和反应20min然后加入规定
