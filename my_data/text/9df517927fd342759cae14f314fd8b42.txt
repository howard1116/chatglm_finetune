摘要：以异佛尔酮二异氟酸酯（IPDI)和三乙醇胺（TEOA)制备支化型异氰酸酯基（一NCO)封
端的预聚体分别以N-甲基二乙醇胺（MDEA)和聚乙二醇（PEG)为亲水扩链剂最后用二甲基吡唑
（DMP)封闭剩余的一NCO基团得到阳离子水性封闭型聚异氰酸酯交联剂，并用傅里叶变换红外光谱
（FT-IR）表征了交联剂的结构-主要研究了预聚反应时异氟酸酯指数（R值）以及亲水扩链剂的种

MDEA为亲水扩链剂、R值为1.9时交联剂的性能达到最佳。

Abstract: A NCO  terminated branched prepolymer was synthesized by the reaction of iso-
phorone disocyanate ( IPDI) and triethanolamine ( TEOA) , then reacted with N  methyldi-
ethanolamine ( MDEA) or polyether glycol ( PEG) as hydrophilic chain extender ,finally reacted
with dimethylpyrazole ( DMP) to block the residual NCO groups to form a cationic waterborne
blocked polyisocyanate crosslinker. Its structure was characterized by Fourier transform infrared
spectrum ( FT  IR) . The effects of isocyanate index ( R,) of the prepolymerization reaction and
the type of hydrophilic chain extender on the properties of the obtained crossliker were dis-
cussed. It was found that the type of hydrophilic chain extender has a significant influence on
deblocking temperature of crosslinker. Thus , the crosslinker showed the optimal performance
when the R, was 1. 9 and MDEA was used as hydrophilic chain extender
Key Words: cationic;waterborne;blocked polyisocyanate;crosslinker
