and toluene disocyanate ( TDI)  were synthesized. The two-component waterborne shape memory polyurethanes
( 2KWPUs) were formulated with water-dispersible polyisocyanate crosslinker and the WPUs. The fabrics coated
with the 2KWPUs were prepared. The influences of molecular of PCL and dosage of the crosslinker on thermal
properties of 2KWPU films and on the thermal-sensitive water vapor permeability ( WVP) of the coated fabrics
were investigated. The results showed that the coated fabrics exhibited significant thermal-sensitive WVP with ap-
propriate dosage of crosslinker. The WVP of some coated fabrics increased by 4  8 times in the temperature range
35  50 °C and the static water
vas higher than 5 kPa
